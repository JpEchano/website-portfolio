Project Overview

Download the app on your Android device so you can take Chikka with you wherever you go. Simply sign up for free using your mobile number to start texting for free. You can also sign up using Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus or LinkedIn so you don't have to worry about forgetting your password again.

And when you're not on mobile, Chikka is also always accessible on the web.

This version gives you more free texts and other cool features to help you conveniently connect with your friends wherever you may be. Happy texting!

NEW: Now, have fun talking with all your friends in Group Chat!

Features

  • More free texts to the Philippines daily
  • Easy sign-in using Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus or LinkedIn
  • Exchange voice messages with fellow Chikka users
  • Sync Chikka with your local Contacts and have them available even on the web
  • More space for longer messages
  • Offline Messaging for Smart & Sun subscribers
  • Automatic 30-day Message Archiving that synchronizes messages in all your devices
  • Export threads or messages to keep memorable conversations
  • Keep friends updated real-time with your status message
  • Personalize your account with a profile photo
