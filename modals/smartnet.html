Project Overview

No more tricks needed to access your favorite social networking sites, chats, email and more only from Smart. No need for proxy, IP's, Opera Mini, UC Browser HUI mods and tweaks on your mobile configuration needed just to access free chat on Facebook, Yahoo Messenger, Mail, Twitter and other Yahoo Services.

All is made easy by Smart using their latest free portal app 'Smartnet', allows you to connect to your favorite sites without worrying of mobile internet charges.
