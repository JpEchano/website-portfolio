What Is This System?

The Clinic Management System is a simple digital system that helps clinics manage patients, appointments, doctors, and medical records in one place. It replaces paper files and manual tracking, making daily clinic operations faster and more organized.

Who Is It For?

Owners

Monitor daily operations and performance.

Staff

Schedule appointments without conflicts.

Doctors

View schedules and patient history.

Patients

Experience smoother, faster service.

Key Features

Scheduling
  • Book/Cancel appointments
  • Double-booking prevention
  • Status tracking
Patients
  • Contact details storage
  • Full treatment history
  • Quick search by ID
Doctors
  • Profile management
  • Specialty tracking
  • Availability view
Consults
  • Diagnosis recording
  • Prescription notes
  • Medical history
Pharmacy
  • Medicine records
  • Availability checks
  • Duplicate prevention
Alerts
  • Change notifications
  • Status updates
  • Read/Unread marks
